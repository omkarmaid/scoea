About Us

Welcome to Shri Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj College of Engineering, a prestigious institution dedicated to providing quality education and empowering the engineers of tomorrow.

Established with a vision to foster innovation, excellence, and holistic development, our college offers a diverse range of programs in engineering and technology. We are committed to nurturing a vibrant learning environment that encourages critical thinking, creativity, and collaboration.

At Shri Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj College of Engineering, we believe in providing our students with a strong foundation in technical knowledge, practical skills, and ethical values. Our experienced faculty members, state-of-the-art facilities, and industry collaborations ensure that our students receive a comprehensive education that prepares them for successful careers in their chosen fields.

Admissions

Welcome to Shri Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj College of Engineering, where we strive to make the admissions process smooth and transparent for aspiring students.

We welcome applications from individuals who are passionate about pursuing a career in engineering and technology. Our admissions are based on merit and consider a holistic evaluation of each applicant.

We look for academic excellence, aptitude for engineering, and a dedication to personal growth. The selection process includes a combination of entrance examinations, interviews, and counseling sessions.

To learn more about our admissions requirements, important dates, and application procedures, please visit our official admissions page or contact our admissions office. We have a dedicated team ready to assist you throughout the admissions process.

We believe in providing equal opportunities and promoting diversity in our student community. We encourage students from all backgrounds to apply and be a part of our college, where they can unleash their potential and shape their future.
